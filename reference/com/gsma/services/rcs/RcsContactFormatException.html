Class Overview

Rcs contact format exception. This exception is thrown when the contact format is not supported or not well formatted. The supported formats are:
- Phone number in national or international format (e.g. +33xxx).
- SIP address (eg. "John" ).
- SIP-URI (e.g. sip:+33xxx@domain.com).
- Tel-URI (eg. tel:+33xxx).
